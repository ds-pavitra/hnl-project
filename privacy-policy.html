Harry & Liu Privacy Policy

We at Harry & Liu Co. Limited respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use our app Timex Fit 2.0, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data

User Profile Information: We collect data that you provide when setting up your account, such as your name, email address, and any other details necessary to manage your health profile

b. Health Vitals Data

Our app collects health-related data, such as heart rate, steps, and other vital health statistics, which are transmitted from your smart health watch via Bluetooth and stored securely on our backend server.

c. Geolocation Data

The app uses geolocation data to enhance the connectivity between the smart health watch and your mobile device. This data is only used to provide a seamless connection between the devices and is not stored or shared for any other purpose.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you, or that you provide to us:
- To operate and improve the functionality of our app.
- To store and maintain your health profile and related data securely.
- To ensure proper connectivity between your smart health watch and the mobile app via Bluetooth and geolocation.
- For customer support and to troubleshoot any issues.

3. Data Storage and Security

- All data collected from your smart health watch, including health vitals and personal information, is stored securely in our backend server, protected with encryption technologies.
- We do not sell, share, or expose any personal information, including health data, to third-party services.
- We maintain strict access controls to ensure that your data is accessed only by authorized personnel when necessary for operational purposes.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share any user data with third-party services for marketing or analytics purposes. Your data remains strictly confidential and is only used for providing the service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your health profile and related data for as long as you maintain an account with us or as required by law. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@harryandliu.com.

6. Your Data Rights

- Access: You can access and update your health profile or personal data within the app settings.
- Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
- Correction: You can correct any inaccuracies in your profile or health data by contacting customer support.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please do not use this app or provide any personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.
