Let's Work

INTRODUCTION

Revolutionizing Urban Living Through Smart City Innovations

Welcome to our Smart City Solutions page. At Harry & Liu, we are committed to revolutionizing urban living through innovative technologies and solutions. From electric vehicle charging stations to smart garbage collection systems, solar grids, and industrial IoT solutions, we offer comprehensive smart city solutions designed to enhance efficiency, sustainability, and quality of life

OUR SMART CITY SOLUTIONS

Electric Vehicle Charging Stations

Our EV charging stations are equipped with hardware and smart software integration, enabling efficient charging management, payment processing, and remote monitoring

Smart Garbage Collection Systems

Our systems utilize IoT sensors and communication technology to track and monitor garbage accumulation in real-time. Automated alerts notify waste management authorities for timely collection, optimizing waste management processes

Solar Grids

We design and implement solar energy systems to power public infrastructure, streetlights, and buildings, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources and lowering carbon footprint

Industrial IoT Solutions

Our IoT solutions for industrial purposes include remote monitoring, predictive maintenance, and asset tracking, enhancing operational efficiency and productivity in manufacturing and logistics

Why Choose Us

Integrated Solutions

We provide end-to-end smart city solutions that address a wide range of urban challenges. Our integrated approach ensures seamless connectivity and interoperability across different systems for maximum efficiency.

Cutting-Edge Technology

Leveraging the latest advancements in IoT, AI, and renewable energy, we develop and deploy innovative solutions that pave the way for a smarter, greener future.

Customization

Every city has unique needs and requirements. We work closely with local governments, urban planners, and stakeholders to tailor our solutions to specific challenges and objectives, ensuring maximum impact and value.
